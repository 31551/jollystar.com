  About ZhuoruiIntroduction
You are here: Home- About Zhuorui Optics

Located in Beijing, the capital of China, Zhuorui is a leading designer and manufacturer of many kinds of optical lenses&optical systems. With a professional design team and a large group of technical talents, Zhuorui has designed and manufactured a series of F-theta lenses, beam expanders and customized optical lens, which are of high quality and reasonable price.

Established in 2007, we have been focusing on optical lens for 10 years. “Customer first; quality is life” is our core value. With these in mind, we will everlastingly offer the best services to customers.

Our customers primarily include companies in laser marking, the semiconductor equipment, automotive and automotive supplier, medical technology, defense and security as well as the aviation industries.
